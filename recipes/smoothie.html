Smoothie

Description of Recipe

This smoothie is a delicious blend of ripe berries, perfect for a nutritious start to your day or a refreshing mid-day treat. The rich colors and smooth texture make it not only tasty but also visually appealing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the mixed berries, banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and honey.
  2. Add a handful of ice cubes to the blender.
  3. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a tall glass, garnish with a mint leaf if desired, and enjoy immediately.

Feel free to adjust the ingredients to suit your taste
